1 on 1 or Group Training

A great way for athletes to enroll in our training program without their organization. We always encourage our athletes to participate in group training for support and motivation during high intensity training bouts. We find the competitive environment supports a higher level of training and are far more cost effective to the athlete as well; however we do provide 1 on 1 session to athletes that would feel more comfortable. All athletes participating in Boothby Programs must be serious athletes looking to create serious adaptations through Hard Work and Effort! Wellness Programs

Are designed to apply the same training methodology to a health enthusiast trying to not enhance performance, but rather slow the aging process. The same athletic qualities that are responsible for higher performance ability are also responsible for quality movement patterns later in life. We want to help our wellness clients stay active through increased lean tissue, increased cardiovascular capacity, and improved core muscle function to help with postural integrity and the always important function of delivering oxygen.
